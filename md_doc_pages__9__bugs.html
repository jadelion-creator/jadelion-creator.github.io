Timeline Mixer Documentation
Found a Bug or Have a Feature Request?

If you've encountered a bug or have a feature request, please contact me at timelinemixer@gmail.com.

For bug fixes and support, I may need to ask a few questions or see a screenshot of your TimelineAsset layout, depending on the issue.

  • If the issue occurs with both Timeline Mixer and a standard Playable Director, it's likely a Unity Editor limitation.
  • If the issue happens only in Timeline Mixer but works in Playable Director, you've found a bug! 🦋

If you're unsure, don't hesitate to reach out—I'm happy to help!

📫 Contact Policy

I welcome genuine messages from users, developers, and curious minds. To help keep communication clean, productive and fair, please note:

💡 Feedback and suggestions

Constructive feedback is always welcome — especially if it helps improve the experience for all users. I genuinely want Timeline Mixer to be the best it can be. If you have ideas or feature requests, feel free to share them. While I can’t implement every suggestion, I do take them seriously.

❓ Questions about features

Not sure if Timeline Mixer is the right fit for your project? I'm happy to answer any questions about features and capabilities.

😁 Working on something cool? I'd love to hear about it!

I always enjoy seeing how Timeline Mixer is used in the wild. If you're working on a fun or unique project and want to share how Timeline Mixer fits into it, I'm happy to chat! Whether it’s inspiration, a demo, or just a friendly discussion — feel free to reach out.

🎟️ Vouchers and Free Access

Requests for free access or vouchers — especially those made under false pretenses and/or in exchange for favorable reviews — will not be considered. Please understand that Timeline Mixer represents significant time, energy, and expertise. Free distribution undermines the work, support, and continued improvements it depends on.

‍Vouchers are offered solely at my discretion. I do not exchange them for reviews — this violates Asset Store policy and my personal ethics.

🚫 Spam & Fraud

Messages involving impersonation, manipulative flattery, fake identities, or suspicious links will be ignored or reported and / or referred back to this page. Attempts to game the voucher system, resell assets, or solicit under false pretenses are taken seriously and forwarded to relevant parties where appropriate.

If you’re reaching out for the right reasons, I’ll do my best to help.
